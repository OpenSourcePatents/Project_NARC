# Legal Authorities

## Statutory and Executive Authorities Supporting the Total Kill Chain Architecture

---

## Criminal Statutes

| Authority | Citation | Application |
|---|---|---|
| Controlled Substances Act | 21 U.S.C. 802-971 | Foundation for all drug enforcement |
| Distribution resulting in death | 21 U.S.C. 841(b)(1)(C) | 20-year mandatory minimum for dealers linked to fatal ODs |
| Conspiracy to distribute | 21 U.S.C. 846 | Network-level prosecution of distribution organizations |
| Money laundering | 18 U.S.C. 1956-1957 | Financial crimes charges against launderers and financial facilitators |

## Data Access Authorities

| Authority | Citation | Application |
|---|---|---|
| Stored Communications Act | 18 U.S.C. 2703 | Legal process for social media platform data (warrants, court orders) |
| Administrative subpoenas | 18 U.S.C. 3486 | Drug investigation subpoenas for financial and communications records |
| Right to Financial Privacy Act | 12 U.S.C. 3401-3422 | Court orders for payment platform records (Cash App, Venmo, Zelle) |

## Sanctions and International Authorities

| Authority | Citation | Application |
|---|---|---|
| IEEPA | 50 U.S.C. 1701-1707 | International Emergency Economic Powers Act, tariff and sanctions authority |
| Drug trafficker sanctions | E.O. 14059 | Sanctions on foreign persons in global illicit drug trade |
| Fentanyl precursors as WMD | E.O. 14367 (Dec 2025) | Designation unlocking WMD-related authorities and resources |
| 1988 UN Convention | International treaty | Precursor chemical scheduling framework, INCB authority |

## Appropriations

| Authority | Citation | Application |
|---|---|---|
| FY2026 counter-fentanyl | P.L. 119-75, Section 7036 | Minimum $150M directed for countering fentanyl from China and Mexico |
| HIDTA Program | FY2025 budget | $290M for federal/state/local task force coordination |

## Proposed Authorities (Not Yet Enacted)

| Proposal | Model | Application |
|---|---|---|
| Platform mandatory reporting | 18 U.S.C. 2258A (CSAM analog) | Require social media platforms to report suspected drug dealing to LE |
| Platform accountability | 47 U.S.C. 230 amendment | Modify Section 230 to address drug trafficking on platforms |
