## Scale Indicators

- TRM Labs (2024): 120+ Chinese precursor manufacturers identified across 26 cities and 16 provinces.
- 97% of identified manufacturers accept cryptocurrency payment.
- Crypto payments to Chinese precursor manufacturers increased 600% from 2022 to 2023, exceeding $26M.
- One group of suspected China-based chemical traders received $37.8M in cryptocurrency from 2018-2023.
- One Mexican chemical broker imported enough precursors for over 2 billion doses of fentanyl for CJNG.

## Regulatory Status

- December 2025: Trump administration designated fentanyl precursors as Weapons of Mass Destruction (E.O. 14367).
- November 2025: PRC placed export controls on 13 precursor chemicals to North America.
- June 2025: PRC completed scheduling all fentanyl precursors scheduled by INCB.
- U.S. has indicted dozens of PRC citizens and companies over the past decade.
- Enforcement described as "uneven and opaque" by 2025 INCSR.
