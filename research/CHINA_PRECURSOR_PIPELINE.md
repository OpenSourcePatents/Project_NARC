# China Precursor Pipeline

## The Source of the Fentanyl Supply Chain

---

## Overview

China is the primary source country for illicit fentanyl precursor chemicals and pill pressing equipment, per the U.S. intelligence community's March 2025 Annual Threat Assessment. China's massive chemical and pharmaceutical industries make the PRC a primary supplier of precursors for fentanyl, methamphetamine, ketamine, and nitazenes.

## Key Precursor Chemicals

### Category 1: No Legitimate Use Outside Fentanyl Production

These chemicals have no identified industrial application other than fentanyl synthesis. Any transaction outside DEA-registered pharmaceutical production is illicit by definition.

| Chemical | DEA Status | Notes |

## The Cat-and-Mouse Dynamic

When precursors are scheduled, illicit manufacturers shift to alternatives:
- NPP and ANPP controlled internationally (2017) → shift to 4-AP, 1-BOC-4-piperidone
- 4-AP controlled → shift to halides of 4-AP
- Each round of scheduling is followed by adoption of "pre-precursors" and novel chemical routes

Multiple synthesis pathways exist (Janssen, Siegfried, Gupta methods), each using different starting materials. Traffickers exploit whichever route uses the least-regulated chemicals.

## Chinese Government Response

### Actions Taken
- 2019: Class-wide scheduling of fentanyl-related substances (curtailed direct fentanyl exports)
- June 2025: Completed scheduling all INCB-scheduled fentanyl precursors
- July 2025: Designated nitazene-class substances for domestic control
- November 2025: Export controls on 13 precursor chemicals to North America
- Dissolved Hubei Aoks and arrested nationals (parallel to U.S. indictment)
- Reported shutting down 140,000 illegal advertisements, ordering 14 platforms to correct

### Persistent Gaps
- 2025 INCSR: PRC enforcement "remains uneven and opaque"
- PRC "has in many instances not acted against companies selling non-scheduled precursors"
- Former senior DHS official: "We couldn't get the Chinese on the phone to talk about fighting child pornography, let alone fentanyl. There was zero cooperation."
- State-owned company (Gaosheng Biotechnology) sold precursors on 98 websites. Provincial officials praised its economic contributions.
- Prison-owned company (Yafeng) operated English-language websites selling fentanyl products to Americans.
- Indicted companies continue advertising post-indictment (Lihe Pharmaceutical still listed on e-commerce platforms as of 2025).

## Emerging Secondary Source: India

India is emerging as both a precursor chemical supplier and a finished fentanyl source. DEA has documented cases of India-based suspects shifting production from China to India due to Chinese regulatory pressure. The pipeline may expand beyond currently identified countries as traffickers diversify.

## Implication for Total Kill Chain

Category 1 precursors (no legitimate use) enable binary enforcement: detect it outside a DEA-registered facility and it's illicit. Period. No probabilistic scoring needed.

Category 2 precursors (dual-use) require anomaly detection: TRADE TRAP and PRECURSOR WATCH modules track shipping patterns, buyer profiles, and financial flows to distinguish legitimate chemical commerce from illicit precursor trafficking.

The precursor supply chain is the single exploitable bottleneck in the fentanyl economy. Everything else (labs, leaders, distribution) is replaceable. The chemicals are the constraint.
