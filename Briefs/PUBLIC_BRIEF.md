## What It Does, Simply

A Chinese company advertises fentanyl ingredients online. A cartel pays with Bitcoin. A mislabeled package crosses the border. A pill gets pressed. A dealer posts on Snapchat. A kid dies.

Every one of those steps leaves a data trail. Right now, nobody connects them. This system connects them.

## The Five Components

1. **PROJECT NARC** -- Real-time overdose radar. Maps every overdose nationally, adjusts for population, and automatically flags communities in crisis before the bodies stack up.

2. **FENTANYL KILL CHAIN** -- Follow the money. Tracks precursor sellers, crypto payments, cartel money laundering, and trade-based laundering from China to Mexico to U.S. streets.

3. **DEALER DOWN** -- Find-Fix-Finish. Scans social media, traces payments, links overdose deaths to dealers, and builds court-ready prosecution packages automatically.

4. **OPERATION TOTAL KILL CHAIN** -- Forces every agency (DEA, FBI, CBP, IRS, state police, local PD, medical examiners) onto one shared platform. No more data silos. No more turf wars.

5. **UNIFIED DOCUMENT** -- Merges all four into a single architecture with a seven-stage workflow, tiered security, and a governance model that ties funding to participation.
